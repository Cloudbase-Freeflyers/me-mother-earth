FAQ

Everything you want to know

Is a stone-type mat safe on bathroom floors?
Yes. Use on flat, even surfaces. The textured top improves footing and helps reduce slips compared to wet tiles.
How do I clean and maintain it?
Wipe or rinse occasionally. For stubborn marks, lightly sand with fine sandpaper to refresh the surface. Avoid harsh chemicals.
Will it crack?
It's sturdy for daily use. Avoid dropping or heavy impacts. Place on a flat surface with the included anti-slip pad.
Does it work in humid bathrooms?
Yes. The open-pore structure releases moisture into the air. Good ventilation enhances speed, but it still dries quickly.

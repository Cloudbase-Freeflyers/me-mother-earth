How it works

3 quick steps to a fresh load

Tear to dose

Perforated for ease. Use ½ sheet for small loads, 1 sheet for medium, and 2 sheets for large loads.

2

Toss in

Top-loaders: place sheet on top of laundry. Front-loaders: tear up and place in the detergent drawer.

3

Wash

Run your cycle. The enzymes help break down stains while the sheet dissolves cleanly in hot or cold water.

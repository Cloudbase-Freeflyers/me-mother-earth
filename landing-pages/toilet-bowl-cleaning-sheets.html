FAQ

Questions about Toilet Bowl Cleaning Sheets

Quick answers to help you get the best results.

What are toilet bowl cleaning sheets? +

Toilet bowl cleaning sheets are a dry, lightweight alternative to liquid toilet bowl cleaners. Tear off a portion, place it in the bowl, scrub as needed, and flush.

How do toilet bowl cleaning sheets work? +

The sheet dissolves in water and activates plant-based cleansers that help loosen residue and tackle everyday toilet bowl stains during scrubbing.

How many cleans are in one box? +

One box includes 12 full sheets. Each sheet is perforated into three portions, providing up to 36 toilet cleans.

Do these replace liquid toilet bowl cleaners? +

Yes. These sheets are designed as a low-waste alternative to traditional liquid cleaners—without bulky bottles or messy pouring.

Are these sheets scented? +

Yes. They have a light citrus scent from lemon peel oil for a fresh, clean finish.

Do I need to use the entire sheet at once? +

No. Each sheet is perforated into three portions, so you can tear off one portion per clean for a pre-measured, controlled use.

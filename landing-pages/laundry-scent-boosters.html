Common Questions

What are scent booster beads?

Scent beads are small, mineral-based pearls designed to dissolve in the wash and infuse fabrics with long-lasting fragrance.

Our formula is plant-derived and free from harsh dyes or phosphonates, making it gentler than traditional laundry fragrances.

Under proper storage, the friction-activated scent particles can keep laundry smelling fresh for up to 12 weeks.

FAQ

Questions about Toothpaste Tablets Refills

Quick answers to help you get the best results.

What are toothpaste tablets? +

Toothpaste tablets are a solid, plastic-free alternative to tube toothpaste. Chew one tablet until it foams, then brush as usual. You get the same fresh, foamy clean without the plastic tube.

What’s the difference between the refill pouch and the jar? +

The refill pouch is a 3-month supply (195 tablets) in a compostable kraft pouch — no jar included. Pour the tablets into your existing refillable jar or any sealed container. It’s the economical way to keep your routine going without buying a new jar each time.

Which formula should I choose — N-HA or Fluoride? +

Peppermint with N-HA uses nano-hydroxyapatite (fluoride-free) for a smooth, clean feel. Peppermint with Fluoride contains 0.32% sodium fluoride, trusted for daily cavity protection. Strawberry with N-HA is fluoride-free with a natural strawberry flavor. Choose based on your preference and dental needs.

How do I use the refill pouch? +

Open the compostable pouch and pour the tablets into your refillable glass jar or any sealed, repurposed container. Store in a cool, dry place. Use one tablet per brush — chew, then brush after meals or twice daily.

Is the refill pouch packaging plastic-free? +

Yes. The 3-month refill comes in a compostable kraft paper pouch — no plastic wrap, no plastic liner. Mother Earth Approved.

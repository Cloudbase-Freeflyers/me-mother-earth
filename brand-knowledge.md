

# **Me Mother Earth Brand Knowledge**

## **1\. Brand Identity & Visual Standards (Me Mother Earth)**

* **Brand Style:** The aesthetic must remain Minimal, Clean, and Simple.  
* **Primary Palette:** Sage Green (\#A1AB97), Off-White (\#EDEDED), Cream (\#ECDFC9), and Dusty Green (\#BBC4B2).  
* **Secondary Palette:** Slate Gray (\#B8C1CE), Forest Green (\#5E704C), and Pale Blue (\#B8C1CE).  
* **Typography Hierarchy:**  
  * **Main Titles:** Use Abril Fatface (Primary Serif) or Shrikhand (Accent/Bold).  
  * **Body & Subtitles:** Use Glacial Indifference, Montserrat, or Helvetica Now.  
  * **Accents:** Beautiful (Script) or Trocchi (Serif).

## **2\. Compliance & Copy Guardrails**

* **The "No-Natural" Rule:** NEVER use the word "Natural" in any design copy or callouts.  
* **Approved Alternatives:** Use "Plant-derived surfactants," "Mineral-based," or specific ingredient names instead.  
* **Ingredient Transparency:** When labeling features, use Sodium Coco Sulfate, Decyl Glucoside, Saponins, Sodium Carbonate, and Sodium Citrate.  
* **Shipping Logic:** Do not mention "Free Shipping over $55" if the product price is significantly lower, as this creates a conversion barrier.

## **3\. High-Performance Visual Frameworks**

* **Action-Oriented Insets:** Use circular "zoom-in" images to provide visual evidence of efficacy, such as showing root health or 90-degree sensor paths.  
* **Functional Iconography:** Use clean, branded icons to explain technical features (e.g., Vibration, Heat, or Soil-Free).  
* **Status Indicators:** Use color-coded icons (e.g., Blue/Green/Red/Blinking) to show product modes or charging states.  
* **Value Density:** Clearly visualize "Everything Included" by using bold quantity multipliers (e.g., 3X, 15X, 2X).  
* **What's In The Box:** Use thin leader lines to identify every component, including accessories and packaging, to build trust.  
* **Lifestyle Feature-Focus:** On lifestyle shots, place 3–4 circular feature breakouts at the bottom of the frame to link "vibe" to specific utility.

## **4\. Post-Production & Technical Specs**

* **Resolution:** Deliver in Amazon-ready high-res (2000×2000 px minimum).  
* **Color Profile:** All assets must be in sRGB.  
* **Consistency:** Maintain consistent exposure, white balance, and color tone across the entire set.  
* **Shadows:** Add subtle shadows for depth; avoid heavy vignettes or high-contrast overlays.
